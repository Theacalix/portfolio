Web Design

Museum Website

Goal

Create an information-based home page for a museum website where users can find info about the museum and ideally purchase tickets to attend the museum. I created a new (fake) museum for this project, so a sub-goal was to attract the target audience to come to the new museum.

Problem

  • Prominent call to action: purchase tickets
  • Promote new museum to attract the target audience
  • Ensure visitors find important info about the museum

Solution

Since this was a fashion illustration museum, my target audience is historical costuming and fashion enthusiasts. I created a gala event for visitors to dress up as part of the grand opening with a link to more info about the event. Below that is the current exhibit and the call to action to purchase tickets. Following common museum design patterns, I added links to other resources like other exhibits, the digital collection, and education outreach. The footer has important info like hours and prices as well as secondary calls to action with a newsletter and donate button so that every page on the site will have this info.

Workflow

  • Research museum websites and design patterns
  • Create personas for target audience
  • Thumbnail sketches of website layout
  • 3 rounds of peer review feedback on the design of the site
